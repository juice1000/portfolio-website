fluentAI

An advanced automation tool infused with popular AI tools, offering unparalleled customization options for streamlined and efficient processes.



Presenting an advanced automation tool engineered by a collaborative team, integrating AI APIs to enable seamless customization of flow processes.

This feature-rich tool encompasses essential core functionalities, including JSON reader, File Input, Template, Text Input, Preview, and Download. The user can also write custom functions in Javascript to further customize their flows. Leveraging cutting-edge AI APIs such as ChatGPT, Dall-E, and DeepL, the tool empowers users with intelligent automation capabilities. Multiple flows are conveniently organized and accessible through intuitive tabs, ensuring efficient flow management. Users can effortlessly copy and share flows, expanding collaboration opportunities. By inviting collaborators with sharing permissions, users can grant them editor rights, fostering collaborative editing and real-time feedback.

The tool also supports live collaboration, facilitating simultaneous editing and interaction among all users with whom the flow has been shared.

VibeVote

A dynamic live voting App for Spotify playlists, empowering event attendees to actively shape the music selection in real-time.



Introducing an exceptionally robust Angular-powered application designed to address the common challenge of relinquishing control from a single individual linking their phone to the party speakers.

This innovative solution empowers every party attendee by providing them with the opportunity to select songs for the occasion. Upon signing up, a user can effortlessly create a new playlist and invite others to join the party through a sharing link. The app incorporates a voting system that not only elevates songs to a priority queue but also determines the next track to be played.

Leveraging my expertise, I contributed significantly to the development of this technically intricate and highly reliable application, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable party experience for all attendees.

Presenting a cutting-edge music application meticulously engineered from the ground up, showcasing my expertise in app ideation, design, and development.

Strong points of this app are rapid song loading, live audio manipulation, and an intuitive interface, ensuring optimal performance and usability.
